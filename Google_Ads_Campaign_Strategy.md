# Google Ads Campaign Strategy

## Target Audience Segmentation
1. **Demographics**: Focus on age, gender, and income levels relevant to the product.
2. **Interests**: Identify interests that align with the product offerings.
3. **Behavioral**: Target users based on previous online behavior, including past purchases.

## Bidding Strategies
- **Maximize Conversions**: Automatically set bids to help get the most conversions within a budget.
- **Target CPA**: Set a target cost per acquisition to gain conversions at a specific cost.

## Ad Formats
- **Search Ads**: Utilize keyword-targeted ads to appear in search results.
- **Display Ads**: Use visual ads on the Google Display Network to create brand awareness.

## Performance Metrics
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Measure the effectiveness of the ad copy and targeting.
- **Cost Per Click (CPC)**: Track the average cost spent for each click.
- **Conversion Rate**: Analyze the percentage of users who complete the desired action.

## Implementation Steps
1. Set up the Google Ads account and configure billing information.
2. Create the campaign structure and select the appropriate ad formats.
3. Monitor performance metrics and optimize the campaign periodically.
